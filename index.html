Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

bangladesh 2.0 will be a place of innovation and creativity for all. It will change and shape the country's future. Innovation will be the key to development

Sustainability

Sustainability is the ability to live and grow in harmony with the environment, and Bangladesh 2.0 will be a place of sustainability

Infrastructure

Bangladesh 2.0 will be a place of infrastructure, Infrastructure will be the backbone of Bangladesh. It will be a place of connectivity

Empowerment

Bangladesh 2.0 will be a place of empowerment, It will be a place of self-reliance and self-sufficiency for all. people will be empowered to achieve their full potential
